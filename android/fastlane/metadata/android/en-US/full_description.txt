ZX Tape Player turns your Android device into a virtual cassette deck for the ZX Spectrum. Connect your phone to a real Spectrum via the headphone jack and load software exactly like you did from a tape recorder — no extra hardware, no modifications.

This is a revival and continued development of the original ZX Tape Player, which was removed from Google Play after its author passed away. The project lives on in his memory, now open source and free of ads and trackers.

HOW IT WORKS
The app converts TAP and TZX tape images into the audio signal a cassette would produce. Wire your phone's audio output to the EAR socket of your ZX Spectrum, press play, type LOAD "" on the Spectrum, and the software streams in just as it would from tape.

SEARCH AND DISCOVER
• Browse over 32,000 ZX Spectrum titles through the ZXInfo database
• View game details, screenshots, publisher information, and release year
• Jump straight from a search result to playback

TAPE SOURCES
• Download tapes directly from Archive.org and other public sources
• Open local TAP, TZX, and ZIP files from your device
• Automatic tape identification by file hash — local files are matched back to their ZXInfo entry

PLAYER
• Animated cassette visualization with spinning reels
• Play, pause, stop, and seek controls
• Adjustable playback speed with tape-recorder pitch semantics (pitch scales with speed, just like a real deck)
• Block countdown timer so you know how long the current block has left
• Adjustable audio output frequency for compatibility with different Spectrum models and clones
• Wake lock keeps the screen on during long loads
• One-tap ringer-mode and volume control for a clean signal
• Warning when a tape image looks corrupted, so failed loads don't leave you guessing

TAPE BLOCK BROWSER
Inspect individual blocks inside a tape file — headers, data blocks, and their contents. Useful for multi-part tapes, skipping straight to a specific level, or understanding the structure of protected loaders.

SUPPORTED FORMATS
• TAP — standard tape image format
• TZX — extended format with copy-protection and custom-loader support
• ZIP archives containing TAP or TZX files

SHARING
Share a downloaded tape or open it in your browser directly from the player screen.

LOCALIZATION
Available in 12 languages: English, Ukrainian, Russian, Polish, Czech, Slovak, Italian, Spanish, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, and Danish.

OPEN SOURCE AND PRIVATE
ZX Tape Player is free and open source. No ads. No trackers. No personal data collected.

Perfect for retro-computing enthusiasts, ZX Spectrum collectors, and anyone who wants to run classic 8-bit software on original hardware.
