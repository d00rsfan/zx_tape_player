ZX Tape Player turns your Android device into a virtual cassette player for the ZX Spectrum. Connect your phone to a real Spectrum computer via the headphone jack and load software just like from a classic tape recorder.

This app is a revival and improvement of the original ZX Tape Player that was removed from Google Play after its author passed away. Development continues in his memory.

HOW IT WORKS
The app converts TAP and TZX tape image files into audio signals identical to those produced by a cassette tape. Simply connect your phone's audio output to the EAR port of your ZX Spectrum, press play in the app, and type LOAD "" on your Spectrum - the software will load just as it would from a real tape.

SEARCH AND DISCOVER
Browse and search over 32,000 ZX Spectrum titles powered by the ZXInfo database. View game details, screenshots, and publisher information. Find the software you're looking for in seconds.

MULTIPLE TAPE SOURCES
- Download tapes directly from Archive.org and other public sources
- Open local TAP, TZX, and ZIP files from your device
- Automatic tape identification via file hash matching

TAPE BLOCK BROWSER
Inspect individual blocks within tape files. See headers, data blocks, and their contents - useful for multi-part tapes or understanding tape structure.

PLAYER FEATURES
- Animated cassette visualization with spinning reels
- Play, pause, stop, and seek controls
- Adjustable audio output frequency for compatibility with different hardware
- Wake lock support to prevent screen sleep during loading
- Ringer mode and volume control for optimal signal output

SUPPORTED FORMATS
- TAP files - standard tape image format
- TZX files - extended tape format with copy protection support
- ZIP archives containing TAP or TZX files

LOCALIZATION
Available in 12 languages: English, Ukrainian, Russian, German, French, Spanish, Italian, Portuguese, Polish, Czech, Slovak, and Hungarian.

OPEN SOURCE
ZX Tape Player is free, open source, and contains no ads or trackers. Your privacy is respected - no personal data is collected.

Perfect for retro computing enthusiasts, ZX Spectrum collectors, and anyone who wants to experience classic 8-bit software on original hardware.
